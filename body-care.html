Body Care for Cats & Dogs

Bathing and Grooming Tips

Regular grooming is essential for keeping your pets clean and healthy. Follow the tips below to maintain their body care routine.

For Dogs:

  • Bathe once a month with dog-friendly shampoo to prevent skin irritation.
  • Brush fur weekly to prevent tangles and reduce shedding.
  • Trim nails every 3-4 weeks to avoid overgrowth and discomfort.
  • Check ears weekly for any debris and use a pet-safe ear cleaner.

For Cats:

  • Brush fur 2-3 times a week to help with shedding and prevent hairballs.
  • Avoid frequent baths; use dry shampoo or pet wipes if needed.
  • Trim nails every 4-6 weeks to prevent scratching injuries.
  • Clean ears gently with a damp cotton pad to remove any buildup.
