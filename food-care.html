Food Care for Cats & Dogs

Meal Times and Foods

Proper nutrition is essential to keep your pets healthy and happy. Below is an overview of suitable food care for dogs and cats.

For Dogs

Provide meals twice a day with a balanced mix of protein, carbohydrates, and fats. Avoid giving chocolate, grapes, or garlic.

Healthy Foods for Dogs

  • Lean meats (chicken, turkey)
  • Carrots and green beans for snacks
  • High-quality pet food with balanced nutrients

Feeding Timetable for Dogs

Meal Time

Dinner 6:00 PM

For Cats

Cats require protein-rich food, primarily from animal sources. Avoid giving onions, garlic, and raw eggs.

Healthy Foods for Cats

  • Cooked meats (chicken, turkey)
  • Small amounts of vegetables like carrots
  • High-quality cat food with essential nutrients

Feeding Timetable for Cats

Allergenic Foods to Avoid

Make sure to avoid these foods as they can be harmful to pets:

  • Chocolate
  • Grapes and raisins
  • Onions and garlic
  • Raw eggs
