Proper administration of medications is crucial for your pet's health. Always follow the guidelines provided by your veterinarian.

Basic Guidelines:

  • Consult your vet before giving any new medication.
  • Use a dropper for liquid medications for easier application.
  • Ensure you are giving the correct dosage and frequency.

Medications for Dogs:

  • Monthly heartworm preventive pills.
  • Flea and tick prevention every 3 months.
  • Regular check-ups and vaccinations.

Medications for Cats:

  • Regular flea prevention every month.
  • Deworming medication every 3-6 months.
  • Consult your vet for any specific medication needs.
